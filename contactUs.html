Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sibol Kakao

Madre de Cacao (Gliricidia sepium) is a remarkable plant with numerous benefits:

  • Natural pesticide properties that protect plants without chemicals
  • Rich in nutrients that enhance soil fertility
  • Fast-growing and sustainable source of organic matter
  • Traditional Filipino farming knowledge combined with modern techniques

At Sibol Kakao, we've perfected methods to maximize these benefits for your gardening needs.
