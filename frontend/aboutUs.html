Our Manufacturing Process

From farm to bottle - transparency in every step

01

Raw Material Sourcing

Madre de Cacao leaves sourced from trusted farming partners in San Miguel, Bulacan. Only mature, pesticide-free leaves are selected to maintain organic integrity.

02

Grinding & Processing

Leaves are ground using dechlorinated water (24-hour standing process) in mechanical blenders, then screened for consistent, fine texture ideal for fertilizer production.

03

Fermentation & Enrichment

5-7 day fermentation process in aerated bins with molasses incorporation to naturally boost nutrient content and enhance fertilizer effectiveness.

04

Sustainable Packaging

Liquid fertilizer strained into reusable glass bottles. Remaining residue dried and packed in kraft paper pouches. All packaging designed for minimal environmental impact.
